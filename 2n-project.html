Overview

The Automatic Street Light project uses an LDR (Light Dependent Resistor) to automatically turn on/off street lights based on ambient lighting conditions.

Components Needed

  • ESP32
  • LDR
  • 4 LEDs
  • 10K Ohm Resistor
  • 220 Ohm Resistor
  • Transistor

Connection Diagram

Why Make This Project?

This project helps in saving energy by automating the control of street lights, turning them on only when it is dark.

Advantages

  • Energy efficient.
  • Automation reduces human effort.

Disadvantages

  • Dependent on sensor accuracy.
  • Requires constant power supply.
